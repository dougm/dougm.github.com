Since

Data Object Description

Describes the data services provided by the storage arrays. In addition to storing bits, some VASA providers may also want to separate their capabilities into lines of service to let vSphere manage finer grain policies. For example an array may support replication natively, and may want vSphere policies to be defined for the replication aspect separately and compose them with persistence related policies.
